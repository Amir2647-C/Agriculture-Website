About Us

Welcome to Rab Nawaz Kamishan Shop, your number one source for all agriculture products. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Rab Nawaz Kamishan, our shop has come a long way from its beginnings. When Rab Nawaz first started out, his passion for providing the best equipment and supplies for his fellow farmers drove him to do intense research, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the country, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the agriculture industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
